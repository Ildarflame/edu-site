Studying is hard enough without ads interrupting your focus playlist, buffering on YouTube tutorials, and paying full price for textbooks. The three biggest entertainment platforms — Spotify, YouTube, and Amazon — all offer massive student discounts that most students don't fully take advantage of.

Here's exactly what you get, how to sign up, and a few tricks to maximize value.

## Spotify Premium Student

[Spotify Premium Student](/deals/spotify-student) costs $5.99/month — that's 50% off the regular $11.99/month Premium plan. But the real deal is what's bundled in.

**What you get for $5.99/month:**
- Ad-free music streaming (100M+ songs)
- Offline downloads
- Unlimited skips and on-demand playback
- **Hulu (with ads) included** — the entire Hulu streaming library
- **SHOWTIME included** — premium shows and movies
- Spotify AI DJ and personalized playlists

That's three streaming services for less than the price of one. The Hulu and SHOWTIME bundle alone would cost $15+/month separately.

**How to sign up:**
1. Go to spotify.com/student
2. Verify through SheerID (enter your school, name, and birthday)
3. Upload proof if not automatically verified (student ID, enrollment letter)
4. Get Premium immediately — verification usually takes minutes

**Tips:**
- You can verify with most accredited institutions worldwide
- The discount lasts up to 4 years (reverified annually)
- If SheerID doesn't recognize your school, try uploading a class schedule or enrollment letter
- The plan auto-renews at full price after graduation — set a reminder

## YouTube Premium Student

[YouTube Premium Student](/deals/youtube-premium-student) costs $8.49/month (vs $13.99 regular) and eliminates every annoyance of free YouTube.

**What you get:**
- **No ads** — ever, on any video, on any device
- **Background play** — listen to videos with your phone locked
- **Offline downloads** — save videos for planes, commutes, offline study
- **YouTube Music Premium included** — ad-free music streaming (Spotify competitor)

**Why it matters for students:** If you watch programming tutorials, lectures, or educational content on YouTube, the ad-free experience is transformative. No more 30-second unskippable ads before a 2-minute coding tutorial.

**How to sign up:**
1. Go to youtube.com/premium/student
2. Verify through SheerID (same process as Spotify)
3. Annual reverification required

**YouTube Music vs Spotify:** If you only need one music service, YouTube Music Premium is included with YouTube Premium Student. You could skip Spotify entirely and save $5.99/month. YouTube Music's library is comparable, and it has the unique advantage of including music videos and live performances.

**The math:**
- Spotify Student + YouTube Premium Student = $14.48/month
- YouTube Premium Student alone (includes YouTube Music) = $8.49/month

If you mainly care about music + ad-free YouTube, YouTube Premium Student is the better deal.

## Amazon Prime Student

[Amazon Prime Student](/deals/amazon-prime-student) costs $7.49/month (vs $14.99 regular) — exactly 50% off. And Prime includes way more than most students realize.

**What you get:**
- **Free 2-day shipping** on millions of items
- **Prime Video** — movies and TV shows (The Boys, Reacher, etc.)
- **Prime Music** — 100M songs (with ads/shuffle on free tier)
- **Prime Reading** — rotating selection of books and magazines
- **Prime Gaming** — free games and in-game content monthly
- **Amazon Photos** — unlimited full-resolution photo storage
- **6-month free trial** — no charge for the first 6 months

**The 6-month free trial is huge.** Most student discounts don't have trial periods. Amazon gives you half a year free before charging the discounted rate. That's effectively 6 months of Prime Video, free shipping, and gaming perks at $0.

**How to sign up:**
1. Go to amazon.com/primestudent
2. Enter your `.edu` email address
3. Verify (instant for most `.edu` addresses)
4. Start 6-month free trial immediately

**Pro tip:** Even after the trial, $7.49/month pays for itself with 1-2 shipping orders. If you order textbooks, electronics, or supplies from Amazon, Prime Student is essentially free.

## Tips for All Student Discounts

**1. Verify early in the semester.** Verification services check enrollment databases. Fresh enrollment data at the start of a semester means smoother verification.

**2. Use your `.edu` email.** It's the fastest verification path for every service.

**3. Set renewal reminders.** All student plans require annual reverification. If you graduate and forget, you'll be charged full price.

**4. Stack discounts.** There's no rule against using all three simultaneously. $22/month for Spotify + Hulu + SHOWTIME + YouTube + YouTube Music + Amazon Prime + Prime Video + Prime Gaming is an absurd amount of value.
