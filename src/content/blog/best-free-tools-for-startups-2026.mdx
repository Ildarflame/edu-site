[Google Cloud for Startups](https://cloud.google.com/startup) offers **$2,000-$100,000 in credits** depending on your startup stage. You also get 24/7 technical support and access to Google's startup network.

[Cloudflare Startup Plan](/deals/cloudflare-startups) gives you the **Pro plan free for one year**, including CDN, DDoS protection, SSL, and web analytics. Great for securing your infrastructure from day one.
