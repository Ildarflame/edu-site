## Common Questions

**Q: How long does verification take?**
Usually instant to a few days. `.edu` emails are fastest. Document uploads may take up to a week.

**Q: Does it renew automatically?**
Yes, you're reverified annually while enrolled. After graduation, access expires (usually at the end of the academic year).

**Q: Can I use these tools for commercial projects?**
Varies by tool. GitHub Copilot and JetBrains — yes. Cloud credits — check individual terms. Most are fine for student startups.

**Q: I was rejected. What do I do?**
Reapply with better documentation. Use your `.edu` email, upload a clear photo of your student ID with current dates visible, and fill out your GitHub profile completely. There's no cooldown — you can reapply immediately.

**Q: I graduated. Can I still get it?**
No. But many individual tools offer post-graduation discounts (JetBrains 25% off, etc.).
