Every startup needs three communication channels: team chat, customer email, and transactional notifications. Here's how to set all three up for free.

## Team Communication

### Slack — Free Pro for 1 Year

[Slack](/deals/slack-startups) gives startups the Pro plan free for their first year. You get unlimited message history (the free plan limits to 90 days), group calls, screen sharing, and unlimited integrations.

After the first year, the free plan is still usable — you just lose message history beyond 90 days.

### Linear — Free for Startups

[Linear](/deals/linear-startups) offers free plans for early-stage startups. If you're managing product development, Linear's issue tracker is faster and cleaner than Jira. Integrates with Slack and GitHub.

### Notion — Free for Students

[Notion](/deals/notion-education) gives students the Plus plan free. Use it as your startup's internal wiki, meeting notes, roadmap, and documentation hub.

## Email Infrastructure

### Mailgun — Student Credits

[Mailgun](/deals/mailgun-students) provides email sending API credits for students. Send transactional emails (password resets, invoices, notifications) reliably with deliverability tracking.

### Twilio — Startup Credits

[Twilio](/deals/twilio-startups) offers credits for SMS, voice, and email (via SendGrid). If your product sends verification codes, alerts, or marketing emails, Twilio covers it.

## Customer Communication

### Intercom — Startup Credits

[Intercom](/deals/intercom-startups) gives qualifying startups credits for live chat, chatbots, and help center. This is the tool that powers the "chat with us" widget on most SaaS products.

### HubSpot — Free CRM + Live Chat

[HubSpot](/deals/hubspot-startups) offers free live chat, ticketing, and chatbot as part of their free CRM. No credit card needed, no limits on contacts.

## The $0 Communication Stack

```
Team Chat:      Slack Pro (free 1 year)
Project Mgmt:   Linear (free for startups)
Docs/Wiki:      Notion (free for students)
Email API:      Mailgun (student credits)
SMS/Voice:      Twilio (startup credits)
Live Chat:      Intercom (startup credits) or HubSpot (free)
```

**Total saved: $3,000+/year**
