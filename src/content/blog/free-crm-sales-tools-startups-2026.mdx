Your first 100 customers don't need Salesforce. They need a CRM that's free, fast, and doesn't require a dedicated admin. Here are the best options in 2026.

## Best Free CRMs

### HubSpot CRM

[HubSpot](/deals/hubspot-startups) offers one of the most generous free CRMs on the market. The free tier includes unlimited contacts, deal tracking, email tracking, and a meeting scheduler. For startups in accelerator programs, HubSpot for Startups offers up to 90% off paid plans.

What makes HubSpot special is the ecosystem — free CRM connects to free marketing tools (email, forms, landing pages) and free customer service tools (ticketing, live chat). You get a complete customer platform for $0.

**Best for:** B2B startups that need marketing + sales in one platform.

### Intercom for Startups

[Intercom](/deals/intercom-startups) gives qualifying early-stage startups significant credits through their startup program. You get their full platform — live chat, chatbots, help center, and product tours.

The key is qualification: you need to be an early-stage startup (under $1M in funding, typically). Apply through their website or via partner accelerators.

**Best for:** Product-led growth startups that need in-app messaging and onboarding.

### Zendesk for Startups

[Zendesk](/deals/zendesk-startups) offers free credits for qualifying startups. The platform covers customer support (ticketing, chat, phone), sales CRM, and analytics.

**Best for:** Startups expecting high support volume from day one.

## Free Sales & Communication Tools

### Slack for Startups

[Slack](/deals/slack-startups) gives startups the Pro plan free for their first year. That's unlimited message history, group calls, integrations, and guest access — everything you need to run your team's communication.

### Twilio for Startups

[Twilio](/deals/twilio-startups) offers startup credits for SMS, voice, and email APIs. If your product sends notifications, verification codes, or transactional emails, Twilio credits save hundreds per month.

### Mailgun

[Mailgun](/deals/mailgun-students) provides free email sending for students and startups — enough for transactional emails during early growth.
