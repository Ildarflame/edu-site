You don't need a designer to build an MVP. With the right free tools, a technical founder can create professional landing pages, pitch decks, and product mockups in hours.

## Design & UI

### Figma — Free Education Plan

[Figma](/deals/figma-education) is the industry standard for product design. The free plan gives you unlimited personal files, and students get the full Professional plan free.

For startup MVPs, Figma is unbeatable: design your app UI, create interactive prototypes, generate CSS code, and hand off to developers — all in one tool. The community has thousands of free templates for dashboards, landing pages, and mobile apps.

**Use it for:** App UI, wireframes, interactive prototypes, design systems.

### Canva — Free Education Plan

[Canva](/deals/canva-education) is perfect for non-designers who need professional visuals fast. Create pitch decks, social media graphics, logos, and marketing materials with drag-and-drop simplicity.

**Use it for:** Pitch decks, social media, logos, marketing materials.

### Miro — Free Education Plan

[Miro](/deals/miro-education) is the go-to whiteboard for brainstorming, user journey mapping, and sprint planning. The Education plan gives students unlimited boards and collaborators.

**Use it for:** Brainstorming, user flows, customer journey maps, sprint planning.

## Landing Pages & Websites

### Vercel — Free Pro Plan

[Vercel](/deals/vercel-pro) deploys your Next.js landing page for free with Pro features for students. Custom domains, analytics, and edge functions included.

### Webflow

Webflow's free plan lets you build and publish one site with a webflow.io subdomain. Good enough for an MVP landing page.

## Prototyping to Code

The fastest path from idea to MVP in 2026:

1. **Brainstorm** in [Miro](/deals/miro-education) (free)
2. **Design** in [Figma](/deals/figma-education) (free for students)
3. **Build** with AI — use [Cursor](/deals/cursor-students) or [Copilot](/deals/copilot-students) to code
4. **Deploy** on [Vercel](/deals/vercel-pro) (free)
5. **Monitor** with [Sentry](/deals/sentry-oss) (free for OSS)

Total cost: **$0**. Total value: **$2,000+/year** in tools.
