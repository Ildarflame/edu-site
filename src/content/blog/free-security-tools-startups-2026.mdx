Security breaches kill startups. The good news: enterprise-grade security tools are free for startups and open source projects. Here's your security stack for $0.

## Error Tracking & Monitoring

### Sentry — Free for Open Source

[Sentry](/deals/sentry-oss) gives the full Business plan free to qualified open source projects. That's 500K events/month, performance monitoring, session replay, and all integrations. Even without the OSS program, the free tier handles 5K events/month.

**Why it matters:** Your first production bug will happen at 2 AM. Sentry tells you exactly what broke, for which user, and shows you the stack trace before anyone complains.

### New Relic — Free via GitHub Pack

[New Relic](/deals/new-relic-students) provides observability for students through the GitHub Student Developer Pack. Monitor your infrastructure, APM, logs, and browser performance.

## Password & Secret Management

### 1Password — Free for Students

[1Password](/deals/1password-students) gives students 1 year free. Share passwords securely with your co-founder, store API keys, and manage team credentials from day one.

### Doppler — Free via GitHub Pack

Doppler provides secret management for developers — sync environment variables across local dev, staging, and production. No more `.env` files in Slack messages.

### Dashlane — 6 Months Free

[Dashlane](/deals/dashlane-students) offers students 6 months of Premium via the GitHub Student Developer Pack. Unlimited passwords, VPN, and dark web monitoring.

## Infrastructure Security

### Cloudflare — Pro Plan Free

[Cloudflare](/deals/cloudflare-startups) gives startups the Pro plan with DDoS protection, WAF, and CDN. Your site is faster and more secure with zero configuration.

### Surfshark — Student Discount

[Surfshark](/deals/surfshark-students) offers VPN with student pricing. Useful for testing geo-restricted features and securing remote work.
