The biggest cloud providers compete to give startups free credits. The reason is simple: get startups hooked early, and they become enterprise customers later. Here's how to stack programs and get $500K+ in total credits.

## Tier 1: The Big Three

### AWS Activate — Up to $100,000

[AWS Activate](/deals/aws-activate) is the gold standard. Two tiers:
- **Founders**: $1,000 credits, free support (self-serve, anyone can apply)
- **Portfolio**: up to $100,000 credits (through approved accelerators, VCs, or organizations)

AWS covers everything: EC2, S3, Lambda, RDS, SageMaker. The credits last 1-2 years depending on tier.

### Google for Startups — Up to $200,000

[Google Cloud](/deals/google-cloud-startups) offers the most generous program. Through Google for Startups Cloud Program:
- **Year 1**: $100,000 in credits
- **Year 2**: $100,000 in credits
- Plus technical support, training, and go-to-market resources

Qualification: Series A or earlier, backed by approved VC/accelerator.

### Microsoft for Startups — Up to $150,000

[Azure](/deals/azure-students) offers $150,000 in credits through Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub. No funding requirement — any startup can apply.

For students specifically: Azure for Students gives $100 free credits with no credit card.

## How to Stack Credits

The key insight: **these programs don't conflict**. You can use AWS for compute, Google Cloud for AI/ML, and Azure for specific services — all on free credits simultaneously.

Realistic stacking for a seed-stage startup:
1. **AWS Activate Founders** — $1,000 (immediate)
2. **Google Cloud free trial** — $300 (immediate)
3. **Azure for Students** — $100 (if you're a student founder)
4. **Microsoft Founders Hub** — $150,000 (apply)
5. **Google for Startups** — $200,000 (through accelerator/VC)
6. **AWS Activate Portfolio** — $100,000 (through accelerator)

**Total: up to $451,400 in cloud credits**

## Best Accelerators for Perks

These accelerators give you credits as part of their program:

- **Y Combinator** — AWS, Google Cloud, Azure credits ($500K+ total)
- **Techstars** — AWS Activate + $100K in partner perks
- **Microsoft for Startups** — direct $150K Azure credits
- **Google for Startups Accelerator** — $200K Google Cloud credits
- **AWS Startups** — up to $100K AWS credits

You don't need to join an accelerator to get credits, but it unlocks the highest tiers.
